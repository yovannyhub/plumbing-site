Why We Left
Power Rodding
In The Past

For decades, the standard plumbing fix was Power Rodding (or snaking)—feeding a metal cable with a blade down the pipe to chop up roots and clogs.

The problem? It's only a temporary band-aid. Rodding punches a small hole through the blockage to let water drain, but leaves thick grease, sludge, and scale plastered to the pipe walls. Within months, the clog rebuilds.

The Hydro Jetting Solution

Blasting water at up to 4000 PSI with specialized 360-degree nozzles, we completely scour the pipe interior. It doesn't just poke a hole; it restores your pipes to a "like-new" condition.

Advanced Jetter Units

Commercial-grade machines capable of delivering precise, sustained pressure up to 4000 PSI to cut through solid tree roots and obstructions.

HD Video Scoping

High-resolution fiber optic cameras allow us to accurately diagnose the pipe condition before and visually verify the clean afterward.

Precision 360° Nozzles

Custom rotating jet nozzles designed to strip away grease and completely decalcify the full inner circumference of the pipe wall.
